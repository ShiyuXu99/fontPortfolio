About this Web

This website is for people who want a basic understanding of Typeface. It specifically introduced each part of the character, and it introduced two important types of fonts: Sans-serif fonts and Serif fonts.
Another intention of the website is to help people who do UI interface design of app and websites to find their suitable fonts.
The fonts I listed on the webpages are carefully choose and they are all suitable fonts for applications and websites, they are excellent in legibility as well as elegancy.
